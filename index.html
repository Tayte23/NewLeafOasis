Mission

A safe, supportive and professional environment where mothers and babies can live together while thorough social work assessments are completed.

What to expect

Transparent, child‑centred and safeguarding‑led practice with clear communication at each stage of the assessment.

Working together

We work closely with local authorities, health professionals and safeguarding agencies to achieve the best outcomes for children.

WHAT WE OFFER

Comprehensive Services

A complete range of assessment and support services, designed to evaluate parenting capacity while supporting families through the process.

Safe, Supportive Living Environment

A structured residential setting where mothers and babies live together during the assessment period.

3‑Week Settling‑in Period

Time for families to adjust to the environment before formal assessment begins.

Parenting Capacity Assessments

Evidence‑based assessment of parenting skills, attachment, insight, motivation and safeguarding awareness.

Risk & Safeguarding Assessment

Identification of risks and protective factors to inform safeguarding decisions and planning.

Structured Observation & Recording

Professional observation of daily interactions, documented thoroughly to support robust reporting.

Court‑Ready Reports

Clear, evidence‑based reports suitable for court proceedings and care planning.
